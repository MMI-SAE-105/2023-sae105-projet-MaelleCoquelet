Alexandre Astier: the Multitalented Creator

  • Reading time : 2 min
  • Written by Maëlle Coquelet

Alexandre Astier is an emblematic figure in French entertainment. For his series “Kaamelott” he managed to surround himself with talented actors. First thought for television, the saga is now a cinematographic tale. With “Kaamelot: the First Chapter” Alexandre Astier proves once again he can do more than comedy. Compositor, actor and film director, his career is an example for many.

Kaamelott’s creator

The most popular creation of Alexandre Astier is of course “Kaamelott”. First broadcasted in 2005, it launched his career as an actor and director. As a comedic tale, the series has conquered the French public. Its sarcastic tone is the trademark of Alexandre Astier. This rewriting of the legend is now iconic, thanks to its creator genius. The series’ six Books follow the adventure of King Arthur, portrayed by Alexandre Astier. His unique view demystified and remodeled the legend. Arthur’s Astier is one of a kind, he’s far from the glorious image described by the legend. He is also greatly inspired by Astier himself. Both of them always wanted kids and find solace in their family. The actor’s parents also play in the movie!

The “Exoconférence” a Dive into the Cosmos

After “Kaamelott”, Alexandre Astier produces, directed and performed a one-man show. He portrays an astrophysicist, tracing back the universe’s life since the Big Bang. The comedian also presents his theories regarding the existence of aliens. We still get his usual comedy, sprinkled with a heavy dose of sarcasm. This was also the opportunity for Astier to put his scientific culture on display. He is indeed a unique celebrity, gifted with a huge curiosity for every subject.

Animation, Astier and Asterix

Alexandre Astier is also known for his two animated films. “Asterix: The Secret of the Magic Potion” is rated as the best movie for the franchise by viewers on Allociné. Premiered in 2018, the movie demonstrates Astier’s adaptability. Animated movies are entirely different from tv series, and the director succeeded into making it his own. These works also allowed him to practice for the longer format demanded by cinema.

Astier’s Music

Not satisfied with being an excellent actor and director, Astier is also an accomplished musician. He’s the one behind “Kaamelott: The First Chapter” soundtrack. His compositions accompany beautifully the movie. Music plays an important part in the movie, especially for King Arthur’s strategic attack at the end.

To conclude

Alexandre Astier is a multitalented artist. He left a mark in the French cultural scenery. From “Kaamelott” to the “Exoconférence” (without forgetting the Asterix movies!), his work reflects his personality. The creator learned to adapt to his era, and his career is far from over.
