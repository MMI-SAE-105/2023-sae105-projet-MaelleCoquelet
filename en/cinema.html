More Cinema

A watchlist of movies recommended after “Kaamelott: The First Chapter”

    • Director: Alexandre Astier
    • Release date: 2018
    • Genre: 3D Animation, comedy

    Panoramix’ epic tale. Searching for a successor, Panoramix, Asterix and Obelix go on a journey. Perfect to learn more about Alexandre Astier’s work.

    • Director: Peter Jackson
    • Release date: 2001
    • Genre: Fantasy

    Frodon, Gandalf and the Fellowship of the Ring embark on a perilous journey, to avoid Sauron’s return. Perfect for classic fantasy fans.

    • Director: Terry Gilliam et Terry Jones
    • Release date: 1975
    • Genre: Comedy

    The iconic tale from the Monty Python, on a quest for the Holy Grail. For nostalgia ridden fans, and classic Kaamelott comedy fans.

    • Director: Jonathan Goldstein
    • Release date: 2023
    • Genre: Heroic Fantasy, comedy, action, adventure

    A recent adaptation from the popular RPG Dungeons and Dragons. Edgin and Holga are failed thieves, who just escaped prison. They realize the world is in great danger, and it’s up to them to save it. Excellent movie balancing action, adventure and comedy.

    • Director: Zack Snyder
    • Release date: 2006
    • Genre: Peplum, action, war

    The heroic and grueling story of the war opposing Spartans to Persians. Leonidas, king of Spartia, leads 300 men in a seemingly lost in advance battle. A classic for history fans, and an example of epic storytelling.
