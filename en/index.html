3 years passed since “Kaamelott: The First Chapter” premiered in theaters. Alexandre Astier’s movie finally narrates what happened to King Arthur since we last saw him. In this epic tale, we reunite with the classic “Kaamelott comedy”. This quote from the Burgonde King is the proof of it. However, Book V’s themes are still relevant. Among them, war, depression, and the importance of the legend. 12 years ago Kaamelott ended, now it’s finally time to analyze the saga and its surroundings.
