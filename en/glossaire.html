Glossary

List and definition of some of the words.

  • Astrophysicist: scientist focusing on studying the properties of celestial objects in the universe, more particularly their origin and evolution.
  • Burgonde: Oriental German people. Their kingdom used to overlap the Bourgogne Franche-Comté region.
  • Britain (in this context): Arthur’s Kingdom, which includes present times Great Britain, Irland and some of the (French) Bretagne coast.
  • Logres Kingdom : also Kaamelott's Kingdom, territory of king Arthur's federation.
  • RPG: role play game. A tabletop or online game in which players portray a character, lead by a Dungeon Master (DM).
  • Salsify: edible root vegetable, tastes a little sweet.
  • Spoilers: key points in a story, telling them can spoil some scenario surprises.
